Who Am I?

Hey there, I’m Thom Crowe, a Tulsa-based dad, deacon, community manager, and all-around nerdy guy. I love being a family man and spend most of my free time with my wife and kiddo. My faith is my passion; I serve as a deacon in the Holy Orthodoxy Church at my local parish. I work remotely for Serverless, a cutting-edge company building the future of web development, growing and building our community. Basically, I get to work with developers promoting a technology I love, which is how I deployed this blog using Hexo. I’m a hobbyist, aspiring developer.

I love technology, playing with cool, new gadgets, watching movies, reading, and cooking. My have a keen interest in the advancements being made in web technology, particularly on the front-end side of things. I love the direction web design is taking at the moment, and I’m delighted to be a part of this era.

Why Did I Start This Blog?

Fatherhood has been an interesting journey, to say the least. Nothing makes me happier than playing with my daughter or having her snuggle up next to me with a book. I’ve learned more about myself by watching and engaging with her than I’ve learned in all the years before she came home, and I’m learning more about my relationship with God, family, friends, and the world around me each and every day. This site serves as a way for me to process and reflect, share my insight, laugh at my missteps, and record what happens in our family.

What Can You Expect To See Posted?

Lots of listicles. Lots of click bate. Or maybe I’ll write about parenting and fatherhood, new toys I find, movies and music I enjoy, or other random topics that tickle my fancy.
