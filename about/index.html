about

I’m Thom. A little about me, I’m a Deacon in the Holy Orthodox Church and serve my parish, the community guy at Serverless, dad to a sweet baby girl, husband to a great wife who I help with her line of housewares and fine art at weather&noise and her shop. I’m an avid reader, beer aficionado, lover of theology and history, aspiring coffee snob. I live in Tulsa with the most amazing wife, a perfect daughter and our two dogs, Tucker and Buddy.
